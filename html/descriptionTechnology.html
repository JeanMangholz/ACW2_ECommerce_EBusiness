Money

Money is a medium of exchange, and we use money as a means of measuring the worth and value of goods and services(University of Minnesota, N.D) . Before the invention of money physical, people would trade their products and possesions between each other, but now in the 21st century, physical money as a means of transaction of value is becoming obsolete(Regalado, 2012). Even though money and its technologies have changed a lot since metal objects were first used as a means of money and trading as early as 5,000 B.C (Wonderopolis, N.D), money remains an exchange medium regardless of what form it takes (Investopedia, 2018).
